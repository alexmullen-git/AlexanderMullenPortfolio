Pepper Integrations

Human understanding and AI integrations using Aldebaran's 'Pepper'

Overview

Pepper is a humanoid robot widely capable from detecting and understanding human emotions to driving around with situational awareness. Most of my work has been with Pepper's human understanding, working in the Python-based 'Coreographe' programming language from basics in speech understanding to reading facial expressions.

Selected Integrations

Attendance Tracker

Elementary school teacher's assistant to understand students

This project was a deep dive into my interest in human understanding with assistive technology. Through facial recognition and human understanding algorithms, I programmed Pepper to survey a classroom and identify all the students to take attendance, following up with an email to the teacher that includes which students were present along with their detected emotion. While this project was not implemented as a real teacher's assistant, it was my first exposure to CV facial recognition and provided me a great platform to work off of for future implementations; I later developed this program into a greeting robot.

Healthcare Companion

Big Hero 6 Baymax-inspired integration

Taking inspiration from Big Hero 6, I programmed Pepper to act exactly as Baymax. Whenever you say ‘Ow’ it activates, and will walk you through solving your symptoms using voice-to-text, human understanding protocals, a central display, and a diagnosis outline approved by a real doctor. Initial segmentation of this program involved meeting with my friend’s dad (an ER doctor) to segmentalize the body into Care Zones, and then based on location and pain scale rating and follow-up questions, we developed a flowchart-based program to make the proper recommendations, prescribe over-the-counter medications, or contact emergency services. Unfortunately, we could not make Pepper fly or shoot lasers.
