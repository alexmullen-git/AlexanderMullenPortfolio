About

Hi, I'm Alex Mullen, originally from South Florida and currently pursuing a Bachelor of Arts in Economics at Harvard College. My background blends technical expertise and creative strategy—spanning multimedia production, programming, robotics, and engineering. I've contributed to the Harvard Independent Newspaper, the Harvard University Robotics Club (HURC), and the Pine Crest Technology Students Association, earning recognition at both state and national levels.

I’ve founded and managed several successful ventures, including ClosedAI Solutions, an AI consulting and software development firm; Alex-Productions, a multimedia production company; the Pine Crest TILE Chapter, a student-run organization with over 500 members focused on entrepreneurship and leadership; and the Pine Crest Film and Digital Media Club, an internationally award-winning club in videography and photography.
