Notable innovations

A few focused engineering design choices and integrations that made the robot more capable, consistent, or competitive.

Axial-Actuating Wings
Space-saving, rigid design for "pushing wings" that mechanically diverts pushingforce into the chassis, allowing for smaller actuators.
High-Torque Pulling Arm
Strategic motor placement for improved joint rigidity, greater flexibility for torque ratio, lighter base for pull-up maneuver.
Pneumatic Disk Launcher
Use pneumatic actuators to control when disks are launched by flywheel, saving power, space, and unnecessry complexity.
